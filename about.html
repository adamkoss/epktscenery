Description of the Scenery
Katowice Airport scenery for X-Plane 10 is made in Laminar Reserch WED (World Editor) and OverlayEditor. I've been doing the scenery since may 2015. Topography and shape of the scenery is based on satelite view, photos, movies and charts. I try to create this, as good as possible. However, I have fixed a lot of bugs so I aware, I will fix a some bugs in future. Therefore I please be understanding.
Important! The Scenery looks the most correct with default scenery data (DSF files).
The picture below, shows created areas within the Scenery. In the middle is the airport. Around of these, is a default scenery. If you have a default scenery files in your X-Plane, EPKT Scenery will be a full compatible in scenery around (especially: terrain mesh shape, roads and railways).
You can find following areas in the Scenery:
  • Huta Cynku Miasteczko Śląskie (ironworks)
  • Part of Ożarowice town
  • Zendek village
  • Zadzień village
  • Part of Pyrzowice village
  • Airport
